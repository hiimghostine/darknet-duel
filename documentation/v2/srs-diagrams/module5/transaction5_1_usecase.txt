Use Case: Match Result Display (Victory/Defeat, Win Condition)

Description: Allows players to view the outcome of a completed match, including victory or defeat and the specific win condition. The system displays the result immediately after the game ends.

Actors: Player

Preconditions: Game session has ended and results are available.

Main Flow:
1. Game ends and system determines the winner and win condition.
2. System stores the match result in the backend.
3. System displays the match result (victory/defeat) and win condition to all players.
4. Players view the result and may proceed to statistics or match history.

Postconditions: Players are informed of the match outcome and win condition.
