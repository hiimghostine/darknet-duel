Use Case: Payment Integration (for Top-Up)

Description: Allows players to purchase in-game currency (Creds, Crypts) using real money through integrated payment providers. The system processes payments, credits currency to the player's account, and notifies the player of the result.

Actors: Player

Preconditions: Player is authenticated and initiates a top-up request.

Main Flow:
1. Player initiates a top-up and selects the amount/currency package.
2. System redirects player to the payment provider or displays payment modal.
3. Player completes the payment process.
4. System verifies payment with the provider.
5. If successful, system credits the purchased currency to the player's account.
6. System notifies the player of the successful top-up.
7. If payment fails, system notifies the player of the failure.

Postconditions: Player's account is credited with purchased currency if payment is successful.
