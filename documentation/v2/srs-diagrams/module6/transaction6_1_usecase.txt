Use Case: In-Game Currency Management (Creds, Crypts)

Description: Allows players to view their in-game currency balances (Creds, Crypts), earn Creds by playing matches (10 for a win, 5 for a loss), and spend currency on purchases or other in-game actions. The system updates balances and enforces currency rules.

Actors: Player

Preconditions: Player is authenticated and has an account.

Main Flow:
1. Player views their current currency balances.
2. Player plays a match.
3. If the player wins, system awards 10 Creds; if the player loses, system awards 5 Creds.
4. System updates the player's currency balance.
5. Player spends currency on purchases or in-game actions.
6. System checks if the player has sufficient balance and processes the transaction.
7. System updates the player's currency balance and notifies the player.

Postconditions: Player's currency balances are accurate and reflect all earnings and expenditures.
