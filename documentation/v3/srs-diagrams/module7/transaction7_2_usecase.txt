Use Case: Report Management (Submit, Review, Resolve)

Description: Allows players to submit reports about inappropriate behavior or issues, and allows admins/moderators to review and resolve these reports. The system tracks report status and notifies relevant parties of updates.

Actors: Player, Admin, Moderator

Preconditions: Player is authenticated; admin/moderator has appropriate privileges.

Main Flow:
1. Player submits a report describing the issue or inappropriate behavior.
2. System records the report and assigns it a status (e.g., open).
3. Admin/moderator reviews the submitted report.
4. Admin/moderator takes action (e.g., warn, ban, dismiss) and resolves the report.
5. System updates the report status and logs the resolution.
6. System notifies the reporting player and the reported user (if applicable) of the outcome.

Postconditions: Reports are tracked, reviewed, and resolved; all actions are logged and relevant parties are notified.
